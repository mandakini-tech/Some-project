Who We Are

Prerna Transport

Prerna Transport is a trusted transport and logistics business based in Ulhasnagar, Thane, Maharashtra. Since 2015, we have been committed to providing reliable, affordable, and professional vehicle rental and freight services. Over the years, we have built a strong reputation for getting cargo where it needs to go — safely and on time.

Operating out of Akash Colony, Office No. 12, Ulhasnagar – 421005, we serve clients across the Mumbai Metropolitan Region, from local deliveries to inter-city routes via Bhiwandi and into Bombay (Mumbai). Our fleet of 7 vehicles — including Eicher trucks, Ape 3-wheelers, and Chhota Hathi mini trucks — ensures we always have the right vehicle for every job.
